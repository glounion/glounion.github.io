Life Happens . . . Is Your Insurance Enough?

Advantage Assurance

When the unexpected happens will your insurance be enough to cover all of your financial obligations?

Advantage Assurance will help ensure your family has time to get finances in order, protect your credit and provide peace-of-mind during difficult times in the event of death, disability, involuntary Unemployment


Available Protection Plans

  Three Star Two Star One Star Credit Card

Packages Offered

  • Single or joint protection
  • Cancellation of your loan balance up to $50,000 for loss of life
  • Cancellation of your monthly payments for up to 6 months or $6000 if disabled by a covered illness or injury
  • Cancellation of your loan payments for up to 3 months or $1500 in involuntarily unemployed
Your purchase of Advantage Assurance is optional and will not affect your application for credit or the terms of any credit agreement required to obtain a loan. Certain eligibility requirement, conditions, and exclusions may apply. Please contact your loan representative, or refer to the Member Agreement for a full explanation of the terms of Advantage Assurance. You may cancel the protection at any time. If you cancel protection within 30 days you will receive a full refund of any fee paid.
