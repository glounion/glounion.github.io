Nick Spanjer

Nick Spanjer started Alleyworks Photography in 2016 after taking pictures for a few years in and around the Pacific Northwest. Beginning with landscapes of Downtown Spokane and the Canadian Rockies, Nick has begun to expand into other projects in the region. A native of Cheney, Washington, and graduate of Eastern Washington University, Nick learned most of what he knows of photography from his parents, who have been active in taking photos for decades.
